About Me


Hello, I'm Sayumi, a front-end developer based in Canada.

I enjoy attending tech conferences and meetups to stay up to date on the latest trends and technologies in the industry. I also enjoy contributing to open-source projects and participating in coding challenges..

In addition to my technical skills, I have strong communication and collaboration skills. I believe that working closely with other team members, including designers, developers, and project managers, is crucial for delivering successful projects.

In the future, I hope to continue growing my skills as a front-end developer and eventually transition into a role where I can lead and mentor other developers. I believe that the best way to learn is by teaching, and I look forward to sharing my knowledge and experiences with others.
